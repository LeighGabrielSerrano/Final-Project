M E A N I N G

According to (Merriam-Webster, 2023), healthcare is "efforts made to maintain, restore, or promote someone's physical, mental, or emotional well-being, especially when performed by trained and licensed professionals." It is taking care of one's self and making sure that their bodies are healthy and strong. It is not just about physical health, it can be mental, emotional, or spiritual.
