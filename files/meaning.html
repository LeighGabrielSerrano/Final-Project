M E A N I N G

According to (Merriam-Webster, 2023), healthcare is "efforts made to maintain, restore, or promote someone's physical, mental, or emotional well-being, especially when performed by trained and licensed professionals." It is taking care of one's self and making sure that their bodies are healthy and strong. It is not just about physical health, it can be mental, emotional, or spiritual. To learn more, click on THIS link.

Healthcare is the proactive and comprehensive enhancement of well-being through the prevention, diagnosis, treatment, and management of diseases, injuries, and other physical or mental health conditions. It encompasses a range of services aimed at maintaining and improving health, promoting recovery, and optimizing quality of life for individuals across all stages of life.
