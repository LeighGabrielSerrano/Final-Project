About this page!

This page aims to spread awareness towards the arising health problems around the country. There are currently ongoing health problems that are left unaided which is why learning them and their symptoms is a necessity, especially for the youth. Among the numerous modern health issues existing, the three that causes most deaths among Filipinos is the following; Ischaemic Heart Disease, Neoplasms or Cancer, and Cerebrovasular Diseases. All three have one thing in common, its causes consists of drinking, smoking and high cholesterol.
